Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our web development services

How long does it take to develop a website?

+

The timeline depends on the project scope and complexity. A basic business website typically takes 2-4 weeks, while complex e-commerce sites or web applications can take 6-12 weeks. We provide detailed timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide ongoing maintenance and support?

+

Yes, we offer comprehensive maintenance packages including security updates, performance monitoring, content updates, and technical support. Our support packages are tailored to your specific needs and budget.

Will my website be mobile-friendly?

+

Absolutely! All our websites are built with a mobile-first approach and are fully responsive, ensuring optimal viewing and functionality across all devices and screen sizes.

Can I update the website content myself?

+

Yes, we can integrate a user-friendly content management system (CMS) that allows you to easily update text, images, and other content without technical knowledge. We also provide training and documentation.

Do you provide SEO services with web development?

+

Yes, we include basic SEO setup with all our web development packages. We also offer advanced SEO services and ongoing optimization to help improve your search engine rankings and drive more organic traffic.

What is your payment structure?

+

We typically follow a milestone-based payment structure: 30% advance, 40% at 50% completion, and 30% upon project delivery. For larger projects, we can customize the payment schedule to suit your requirements.
