Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our graphic design services

What file formats do you provide?

+

We provide designs in all necessary formats including AI, EPS, PDF, PNG, JPG, and SVG. For print projects, we ensure files are print-ready with proper resolution and color profiles (CMYK). For digital use, we optimize files for web (RGB).

How many revisions are included?

+

Our packages include 2-3 rounds of revisions depending on the package level. For our Professional and Enterprise packages, we offer unlimited revisions to ensure you're completely satisfied with the final design.

Do you handle printing services?

+

While we don't handle printing directly, we prepare all print designs to be print-ready and can recommend trusted printing partners. We also provide print specifications and can liaise with your chosen printer to ensure quality results.

Can you work with our existing brand guidelines?

+

Absolutely! We can work within your existing brand guidelines to create new designs that maintain brand consistency. We can also help update or expand your brand guidelines if needed.

What's your typical turnaround time?

+

Turnaround times vary by project complexity. Simple designs like business cards take 2-3 days, while comprehensive brand identity projects take 2-3 weeks. We always provide realistic timelines during the consultation phase.

Do you provide copyright and ownership of designs?

+

Yes, upon full payment, you receive complete ownership and copyright of all final designs. We provide a copyright transfer document and you're free to use, modify, and distribute the designs as needed for your business.
